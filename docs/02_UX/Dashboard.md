# Vero Volley Partnership OS

# Dashboard

**Version:** 1.0
**Status:** Approved
**Document Owner:** Product Team
**Last Updated:** July 2026

---

# 1. Purpose

The Dashboard is the daily home of every Partnership Manager.

It is the first screen opened every morning and should provide a complete operational overview of the commercial activity.

The Dashboard is not a reporting page.

It is a decision-making page.

Its purpose is to answer one simple question:

> **What should I do today?**

---

# 2. Design Philosophy

The Dashboard should never overwhelm the user with information.

Instead, it should prioritize:

- urgency;
- opportunities;
- relationships;
- recommended actions.

Every widget must help the commercial team make a decision.

---

# 3. Dashboard Layout

The Dashboard is divided into functional widgets.

Each widget answers a specific business question.

---

# 4. My KPIs

This section summarizes the user's performance.

Display:

- Open Opportunities
- Total Pipeline Value
- Proposals Sent
- Active Partners
- Activities Completed This Week
- Meetings Scheduled
- Follow-ups Due
- Invitations Sent

The KPI cards should be clickable and open the related records.

---

# 5. Today's Agenda

Displays everything that requires attention today.

Examples:

- Meetings
- Phone Calls
- Follow-ups
- Proposal Deadlines
- Match Invitations
- Business Lunches

The user should understand today's priorities within seconds.

---

# 6. My Opportunities

Displays only opportunities owned by the current user.

Columns:

- Company
- Opportunity
- Stage
- Value
- Closing Date
- Last Activity
- Next Action

Quick Actions:

- Open Opportunity
- Log Activity
- Send Proposal
- Schedule Meeting

---

# 7. Upcoming Follow-ups

Shows follow-ups approaching their due date.

Display:

- Company
- Contact
- Due Date
- Priority
- Suggested Action

Late follow-ups should appear first.

---

# 8. Upcoming Matches

Professional volleyball is one of the strongest commercial assets.

The Dashboard should display upcoming matches that can be used for:

- Hospitality
- Invitations
- Networking
- Business Community Events

Display:

- Opponent
- Competition
- Venue
- Date
- Remaining Hospitality Availability

Quick Actions:

- Invite Company
- View Guest List
- Create Invitation

---

# 9. Contact Directory

A searchable directory of every contact.

Display:

- Name
- Company
- Job Title
- Email
- Phone Number
- Mobile
- LinkedIn

Quick Actions:

- Call
- Send Email
- Open Workspace
- Log Activity

The directory should support instant search.

---

# 10. Recent Activities

Displays the latest commercial activities.

Examples:

- Meetings
- Calls
- Emails
- Notes
- Match Invitations
- Business Lunches

Each activity should display:

- User
- Company
- Type
- Date
- Outcome

---

# 11. AI Sponsor Research

The Dashboard should include a dedicated AI widget.

Capabilities:

- Suggest potential sponsors
- Detect similar companies
- Recommend industries
- Highlight growing sectors
- Show recent sponsor news

Quick Actions:

- Add Company
- Save Lead
- Assign Owner

---

# 12. Notifications

Displays important events.

Examples:

- Proposal accepted
- Proposal viewed
- Upcoming contract expiration
- Hospitality confirmation
- Assigned opportunity
- Mention by another user

Notifications should always be actionable.

---

# 13. Business Community

Displays recent Business Community activity.

Examples:

- New Partner Joined
- Upcoming Networking Event
- Business Lunch
- Community News

---

# 14. Personal Performance

Displays personal statistics.

Examples:

- Activities completed
- Pipeline growth
- Meetings held
- Conversion Rate
- Opportunities Won
- Average Deal Size

The objective is self-improvement rather than reporting.

---

# 15. AI Daily Briefing

Every morning the Dashboard should display an automatically generated summary.

Example:

"Good morning Ulisse.

You have:

• 3 meetings
• 6 follow-ups
• 2 proposals awaiting feedback
• One partnership at high risk
• One company recommended for the Monza match."

The briefing should be generated from live CRM data.

---

# 16. Search

A global search bar should always be visible.

Users should be able to search:

- Companies
- Contacts
- Opportunities
- Activities
- Documents
- Proposals
- Hospitality
- Matches

Results should appear instantly.

---

# 17. Quick Actions

Always available.

Examples:

+ New Company

+ New Contact

+ New Opportunity

+ Log Activity

+ Create Proposal

+ Invite to Match

+ Search Sponsor

+ Open Calendar

---

# 18. Personalization

Every user should be able to customize:

- Widget order
- Favorite companies
- Favorite contacts
- KPI cards
- Default filters

Managers may have additional widgets.

---

Future releases may include:

---

# 20. Success Criteria

A Partnership Manager should understand their entire working day in less than 30 seconds.

The Dashboard succeeds when it becomes the page users naturally return to throughout the day.

---

# 21. Guiding Question

Every Dashboard widget should answer one question:

**Does this help the commercial team understand what to do next?**

If the answer is no, the widget should be redesigned or removed.
